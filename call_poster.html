Call for Posters

High-quality poster presentations are a vital part of RAID. The poster session provides a platform for discussing works in progress, new research directions, and challenges on various security and privacy topics.

Type 1: Novel Ideas

For original work and contributions

  • Describe main contributions and motivation
  • Include preliminary results if available
  • Maximum 2 pages (including references)
  • Use RAID style format (no copyright block)
  • Must not be anonymized
  • Titles must begin with "Poster:"

Poster titles will appear on the conference website but not in proceedings.

Type 2: Published Research

For papers published in 2023 or later

  • One-page single-column document
  • Full bibliographic reference
  • Abstract of original paper
  • Link/DOI to published paper

Accepted posters will have title and authors on website with link to published work.

Submission Requirements

Poster Draft

All submissions must include a draft poster:

  • Final size: 32×40 inches
  • Submit scaled to letter paper size
  • Feedback provided before symposium

Submission Format

Bundle as a single PDF document:

  • First pages: Poster abstract
  • Last page: Poster draft
  • Example: 3-page PDF = 2 pages abstract + 1 page draft

Submit Poster

Important Notes

  • Submissions based on papers published more than two years ago will not be accepted
  • Posters will not appear in the official proceedings
  • At least one author must present the poster at the conference
